We are thrilled to announce that Heartland has been selected to participate in the prestigious [Amazon Devices Climate Tech](https://solve.mit.edu/amazon-devices-climate-tech-accelerator)Accelerator, an equity-free program designed to propel climate tech innovations into millions of Amazon devices worldwide. This collaboration with Amazon, MIT Solve, and Newlab marks a significant milestone in our mission to decarbonize plastic production and promote sustainable materials in consumer electronics.

## The Honor of Being Selected For Amazon Climate Tech Accelerator

Being chosen for the Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator is a testament to Heartland’s commitment to pioneering sustainable solutions for the electronics industry. This highly competitive program, which closed applications earlier this year, seeks innovative companies focused on decarbonizing critical aspects of device manufacturing, including displays, batteries, printed circuit boards (PCBs), and low-carbon materials like bioplastics and recycled alloys.

Heartland’s focus on decarbonizing plastics aligns perfectly with Amazon’s vision of achieving net-zero carbon emissions by 2040, as outlined in The Climate Pledge. The accelerator offers unparalleled opportunities, including mentorship from industry leaders, access to AWS credits, and the chance to pitch directly to Amazon executives.

## What the Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator Offers

The Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator is not your typical startup program. It is tailored to fast-track climate tech solutions that can integrate into Amazon’s vast ecosystem of consumer electronics. Key focus areas include:

- **Low-Carbon Materials**: Innovations like carbon-captured polyoxymethylene (CPOM), bioplastics, and recycled carbon fiber.

- **Circularity**: Solutions that promote recycling, reuse, and closed-loop manufacturing.

- **Device Efficiency**: Technologies that extend product lifespans and optimize energy use.

Participants receive hands-on support from MIT Solve and Newlab, leveraging their expertise in scaling climate technologies and fostering industry connections. This collaborative environment empowers companies like Heartland to refine our product offerings and gives us an opportunity to earn Amazon business.

## Past Success Stories: Outcomes from Amazon’s Accelerators

Amazon’s commitment to sustainability extends beyond its own operations. While the Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator is relatively new, we can look to the broader Amazon Sustainability Accelerator for inspiring examples of past participants’ success.

- **Suri**: A UK-based sustainable toothbrush company, Suri joined the Amazon Sustainability Accelerator in its concept stage. With the program’s support, Suri scaled its operations, achieving over £10 million in gross revenue within 12 months and gaining access to critical resources and expertise.

- **FUNQ’**: This German and Spanish startup, which produces natural superfruit syrups, leveraged the accelerator to expand distribution to 1,600 stores in Germany and raise over €2 million in capital. These success stories highlight the accelerator’s ability to supercharge growth and amplify climate impact.

Since its launch in 2022, the Amazon Sustainability Accelerator has supported over 25 startups across the UK and Europe, helping them increase sales by 700% and raise over £16 million collectively. While specific outcomes from the Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator are still emerging, the program’s structure suggests similar potential for Heartland. By focusing on decarbonizing plastics, we aim to follow in the footsteps of these trailblazers, scaling our innovations to reduce emissions and promote a circular economy in consumer electronics.

## Heartland’s Vision for Decarbonizing Plastics

At Heartland, we are dedicated to [revolutionizing plastic production](/imperium-masterbatch/) by developing sustainable alternatives that minimize environmental impact. Our participation in the Amazon Devices Climate Tech Accelerator does not guarantee business, but will allow us to explore cutting-edge materials that align with Amazon’s goal of incorporating sustainable materials into devices.
