## Introduction to Hemp-Lyocell Blends

[Hemp-lyocell blends](https://www.thehemploom.com/portfolio/) are an innovative powerhouse in sustainable textiles, fusing the rugged strength, natural antimicrobial properties, and eco-friendliness of hemp with the silky softness, exceptional drape, and closed-loop sustainability of lyocell (often known as Tencel™). At Heartland, our hemp-lyocell blends feature **20–35% hemp content**, delivering a luxurious "silky soft" hand feel that's breathable, dries quickly, and incredibly comfortable, ideal for premium apparel and accessories.

#### Hemp Composition and Percentage

The magic of a hemp-lyocell blend comes from its thoughtful ratio. Heartland blends hemp with lyocell at 20–35% hemp, harmonizing hemp's tensile strength, UV resistance, and natural antimicrobial benefits with lyocell's buttery smoothness, superior moisture management, and elegant flow.

### Hemp Content

[Heartland blends hemp](/imperium-fabric/) with lyocell between 20–35%. This range creates lightweight, robust fabrics with a significantly lower environmental footprint than conventional alternatives, thanks to hemp's low-input growth and lyocell's efficient, solvent-recycling production.

### Hemp Ratio Percent

Around 20% hemp yields exceptionally soft, drapey fabrics perfect for next-to-skin premium casual wear. Up to 35% hemp boosts durability and longevity while preserving the luxurious feel that softens beautifully with each wash.

#### Possible Yarn Counts (Ne)

Yarn count in English Count (Ne) defines the yarn's fineness, weight, and suitability. Heartland currently produces hemp-lyocell blends in counts from **16s–30s**, offering excellent versatility for refined textures and premium applications.

- **16s–24s Counts**: Mid-range yarns ideal for balanced weight fabrics with great strength and subtle texture—suited to structured yet soft garments.

- **24s–30s Counts**: Finer yarns deliver ultra-smooth, lightweight materials with exceptional drape and breathability, perfect for elevated apparel.

This flexibility supports customization, such as "hemp lyocell yarn 30 Ne" for flowing dresses or "16 Ne hemp lyocell blend" for durable yet soft structured pieces.

## Fabric and Yarn Types

Heartland hemp-lyocell blends come in both knit and woven constructions to suit varied manufacturing and end-use requirements.

### Knit Fabrics

Soft, fluid, and comfortable—ideal for premium tees, lightweight activewear, or drapey loungewear. The knit structure enhances airflow, subtle stretch, and skin-friendly comfort.

### Woven Fabrics

Elegant, structured, and resilient—perfect for shirts, dresses, soft tailored garments, bags, or footwear uppers. Weaves like plain, twill, or satin-inspired add sophistication and performance.
